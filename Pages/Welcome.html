A Message from the Producers

Welcome to Jazz to Jive, a unique, exciting evening, the likes of which you won't find anywhere else in Abu Dhabi.

We are delighted to present to you an energetic, toe-tapping, entertaining blend of acappella, big band music and dancing into the wee small hours.

At additional cost, feel free to partake in the foodcart, which will be servrd by The Club from the food cart starting at 7:30, and of course the bar, which will remain open for the entire evening.

If you wish to know more about the performers, the details of each of the groups and website / social media handles are included within this digital program.
