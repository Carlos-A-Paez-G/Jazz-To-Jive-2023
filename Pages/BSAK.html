BSAK Jazz Band

The award winning BSAK Jazz Band began at the British School Al Khubairat in 2022, the "brain child" of Directors All Bryce and Steve Kerr.

BSAK Jazz Band reached the final of the Young Musician of the Gulf Ensemble Competition in 2023, bringing home the trophy for Best Large Ensemble in the Gulf with performances full of character and sparkle. They are always in demand to play, performing recently at the BSME Headteacher's Conference, ADYM Final at NYUAD, BSAK International Cabaret Evening, BSAK Music Awards and Secondary Awards evenings and the "Music from Stage and Screen" Concert, to name but a few.

BSAK Jazz Band has an eclectic repertoire and performs Jazz, Funk, Big Band standards and Pop with energy and enthusiasm! They are extremely versatile, love performing and are very much looking forward to being part of Jazz to Jive at The Club.
