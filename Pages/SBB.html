Saadiyat Big Band

Making its home on Saadiyat Island close to the sunny beaches of Abu Dhabi, the 20-piece Saadiyat Big Band comprises of

talented musicians performing nostalgic and funky tunes from the heady days of the Big Band and Modern eras.
