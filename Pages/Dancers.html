Reverie Dance

Reverie Dance is a dance studio located in the heart of Abu Dhabi near Qasr Al-Hosn, that specializes in teaching on Ballroom dancing and Argentine tango to both adults and children, also variety of dance styles like Lindy Hop, West Coast Swing, Hustle and many other.

At the end of last year, Reverie Dance opened its doors to the world of dance for anyone who wants to learn and explore dancing. Reverie Dance has participated in various Argentine Tango festivals in Catania, Munich, and Porto in both 2022 and 2023, where students showcased their skills and achievements in dancing. Furthermore, Reverie Dance achieved outstanding results in the Dubai Crown Cup and Swedish Open 2023 ballroom competitions, and the impressive performances led to earning medals.

Reverie Dance welcomes anyone who wants to become a part of the dance community and provides a supportive and inclusive environment.
